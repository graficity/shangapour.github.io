Our startup sets out to develop an application that provides citizens, using simple user friendly tools, with a virtual opportunity for sharing their visual content over urban frontages through an augmented reality (AR) technique.

PROBLEMS & OPPORTUNITIES


Graffiti in our contemporary cities, has become so popular as an important means for people to express themselves. B usinesses have also benefited from graffiti as a free advertising medium. Whereas it, most of the time, lacks aesthetic appeal or might be considered as a form of vandalism , graffiti remains as the voice and appearance of those whose opinions, sentiments and intelligence have not been heard and seen.

We believe that technology could make a hybrid space as an innovative opportunity for the self expression of each and everyone in streets while leaving facades and frontages clean and undamaged. Apart from current social networks’ virtual pages, ordinary people equally need to have urban spaces to express themselves as well. Our startup sets out to develop an application that provides citizens, using simple user friendly tools, with an augmented reality (AR) technique for sharing their content over urban frontages. What one virtually writes, draws or paints to share is viewed in the real world through the lens of smart phone cameras and can be commented on and edited. While walking through city areas, people, using our application, can discover, over their phone cameras, a different interesting urban experience reflecting others’ feelings and ways of thinking. Furthermore, businesses of various sizes can enjoy advertising opportunities our application offers.

How It Works?


What one virtually writes, draws or paints to share is viewed in the real world through the lens of smart phone cameras and can be commented on and edited. While walking through city areas, people, using our application, can discover, over their phone cameras, a different urban experience reflecting others’ feelings and ways of thinking.


Using sensors such as accelerometer, GPS, gyroscope, solid state compass, images that have been generated and shared on the application, overlays people’s view of the real urban faces and facades and can be evaluated or even, without being removed, superimposed by new images.

Value Proposition

Where social network, urban space, andaugmented reality meet; this is the magic of Graficity that finally real urban walls and frontages turn into online social platform pages.

Social

Enabling users easily and freely share their opinions, feelings and information visually over urban reality.

Cultural

Creating a new urban experience within everyday life of people, as the site of public encounter, offering visitors a sense of local culture.

Economic

A creative advertising platform for businesses of any sizes to present their products and services.

Political

A democratic venue where those who are excluded assert their rights to have an effect on politicians, policy makers, planners, etc.

Advantages

Our application is unique in its kind, as it engages the social network with real urban spaces through augmented reality . Users can enjoy a deeper understanding of urban inhabitants revealing interesting and informative layers generated by AR.

It is the only application that allows any ordinary user produce its own AR images over city facades. AR apps like Wikitude , AR City, CityLab are limited to only internally created AR images.

This application provides a quite just, fair and open space for any ideas and outdoing social networks platform like Facebook, Instagram that favor famous people more, it shows preference for images based on the importance of the urban surface on which the image over lays and the degree of welcome it receives, not only the name of the producer. So it is quite selective in which creativity matters most and the specificity of images that is being matched to a specific location, direction and size means the minimum risk of copying.

Compared to other social media advertising platform, It provides an impartial space for small businesses as the visual creativity of their advertisement carries more weight than their financial position.

As the activities are location based, it is a new and unique digital social network concept in which local people in any specifi c geographic area get to socialize in streets. Current social networks fail to enable local connections and interactions whereas Graficity can help you know how the people around you feel and think.

There is no application at present fully embracing our concept and offering all abovementioned options at once.
